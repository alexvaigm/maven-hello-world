1. To see the job logs:
  kubectl logs job/{{ .Release.Name }}-job -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2. To check the job status:
  kubectl get jobs -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
  kubectl describe job {{ .Release.Name }}-job -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

3. To see the pods created by the job:
  kubectl get pods --selector=job-name={{ .Release.Name }}-job -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
